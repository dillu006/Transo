Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction

VoiceX respects your privacy and is fully committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Microphone input — Voice audio captured only while you actively press the mic button
  • Speech-to-text data — Text generated from your voice via your browser's built-in Web Speech API
  • Language preferences — The languages you select are stored locally in your browser only
  • Conversation history — Stored temporarily in your browser session only; cleared when you close the app

How We Use Your Data

  • To convert your voice to text (processed locally in your browser)
  • To provide real-time translation via MyMemory API
  • To display your conversation history during your session
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising, profiling, or analytics

Data Storage

  • We do NOT store your voice recordings permanently — anywhere
  • Speech data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded
  • No user accounts, no login, no database — zero server-side storage
  • Conversation history exists only in your browser's memory during the active session

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted APIs to power translation:

  • Web Speech API — Your browser's built-in speech recognition (Google Chrome). Processed by your browser/Google as per their policies.
  • MyMemory Translation API — Free translation service by Translated.net. Text sent for translation is subject to their usage policy.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

User Consent & Permissions

  • By pressing the microphone button, you grant temporary microphone access
  • You can revoke microphone permission anytime via your browser settings
  • Using VoiceX means you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy

Data Security

Since we store no personal data on our servers, your information is inherently safe from server-side breaches. We use HTTPS for all API communications to protect data in transit.

Children's Privacy

VoiceX is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
